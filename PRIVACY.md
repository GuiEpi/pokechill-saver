# Privacy Policy — pokechill-saver

**Last updated:** March 2026

## Data Collection

pokechill-saver does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

## What the extension accesses

- **pokechill game data**: The extension reads and writes the `gameData` key in localStorage on `https://play-pokechill.github.io/` solely to back up and restore your game save.
- **Dropbox OAuth tokens**: Access and refresh tokens are stored locally in your browser's extension storage (`browser.storage.local`) to maintain your Dropbox connection. These tokens are only sent to Dropbox's API servers and are never shared with any third party.

## Third-party services

- **Dropbox**: Used to store and retrieve your game save file. Your data is subject to [Dropbox's Privacy Policy](https://www.dropbox.com/privacy).

## Data sharing

We do **not** sell, transfer, or share any user data with third parties.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please [open an issue](https://github.com/guiepi/pokechill-saver/issues).
